Privacy Policy

Meet Halfway — Effective Date: 17 May 2026

Meet Halfway (“the App”, “we”, “us”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights in relation to that data.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the minimum information necessary to provide the core functionality of the App.

1.1 Account Information

When you sign in, we collect:

  • Email address (via Firebase Authentication)
  • Display name (stored in Firestore)

This information is used to identify your account and enable app functionality such as invites and matching.

1.2 Location Data

The App uses location data to provide its core feature: finding places near a shared midpoint between users. We may collect or process:

  • Approximate or precise device location (only when needed for a session)
  • Location-derived coordinates used to calculate a midpoint between users

Important:

  • Location is used only for generating recommendations
  • We do not continuously track location in the background
  • Location data is not used for advertising or profiling

1.3 User Activity Data

We store limited interaction data such as:

  • Places you view or interact with
  • Swipe or preference actions within the app
  • Session-based usage behaviour

This data is used to improve recommendations, enable matching between users, and maintain session state and app functionality.

2. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide core app functionality (midpoint discovery and venue suggestions)
  • Enable user accounts and authentication
  • Support invitations and shared sessions
  • Improve relevance of venue recommendations
  • Maintain app performance and reliability

We do not sell personal data.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Firebase services (Google Cloud). We take reasonable steps to protect data from unauthorised access, secure data transmission and storage, and limit access to necessary systems only. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data only in the following cases:

  • With Firebase (Google Cloud) as our backend provider
  • If required by law or legal process
  • To protect the safety, security, or integrity of the App

5. Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to provide the App’s functionality, maintain user accounts, and support ongoing app usage. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing (where applicable)

If you are located in Australia, you may also have rights under the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth).

To exercise these rights, contact us using the details in Section 9.

7. Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended for users under 13 years of age (or the minimum age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the Effective Date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App means you accept the updated policy.
